C1ZZL3 Quick Reference
======================

Release: 84 / Rad v9
UF2: ../uf2/C1ZZL3_RAD.uf2
Checksum: d31f2dbf25c633cf6a5943cf288231f32702d255e42f7b70059e9d2804de705a

Stability boundary
------------------
- Turing MIDI output is optional and defaults off.
- Turing CV and pulse outputs continue running in synth mode.
- Tap tempo is removed; Y is the Turing internal clock-speed control.
- Rapid retriggers keep oscillator phase continuous while an envelope is active.

Switch middle: PD synth
-----------------------
Main: pitch
X: phase distortion
Y: waveform
Audio/CV In 1: 1V/oct pitch
CV In 1: adds phase distortion
CV In 2: adds wave control
Audio In 2: unused
Pulse In 2: trigger selected envelope, held gate loop/sustain, gate-off release

Switch down from middle: performance edit
-----------------------------------------
Main: oscillator 2 detune
X: ring modulation
Y: noise/grit
Hold switch down from middle to save current performance settings.
Ring/noise start neutral after reset.
If powered with switch down, ring/noise edit is locked until the switch leaves down.

Switch up: Turing
-----------------
Main: mutation
X: sequence length, 2-16 steps
Y: clock speed
Pulse In 1: external clock
CV Out 1: stepped Turing CV
CV Out 2: smoothed Turing CV
Pulse Out 1: main Turing pulse
Pulse Out 2: alternate bit pulse

Startup envelope select
-----------------------
Hold switch down during startup.
Main selects factory presets and saved custom slots.
Short down press exits.
Long down hold saves selected preset.
LED 6 marks custom slots; LEDs 1-3 show custom slot number.

Open:
http://localhost:5173

Editor supports:

- eight custom envelope slots
- Load RAM
- Load Envelope + Settings
- Save Envelope
- Delete Envelope Slot
- Read Envelopes from Card
- Read Settings from Card
- Send Settings
- browser audition

MIDI CC
-------
CC1: phase distortion
CC20: oscillator 2 detune
CC21: ring modulation
CC22: noise amount
CC23: waveform
CC24: Turing CV octave range, 1-8 octaves

Factory envelopes
-----------------
0 Off
1 Pluck
2 Double pluck
3 Bounce
4 Bell
5 Brass
6 Strings
7 Reverse swell
8 Evolving digital
